D&D Character Rolled Stats Luck Calculator

Discover how fortunate your stat rolls are with this calculator. Quantify the luckiness of your stats and express it in numbers.
Evaluating the value of different stat distributions can be subjective.
For example, rolling {15, 15, 12, 12, 12, 12} is often preferred over {13, 13, 13, 13, 13, 13}, despite having the same total points.
To address this, we assign a score to each stat, reflecting its relative importance.

We've pre-filled recommended scores, but you can also assign equal values to each point or customize your own distribution.







How did you roll for your stats? Did you roll 4d6 and choose the highest 3 outcomes for each stat?
Or did you roll a d20 for each stat? If you used a different method, you can manually enter the chance distribution.
